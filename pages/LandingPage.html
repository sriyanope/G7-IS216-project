About Us

A hub for gardening enthusiasts, environmentalists, and individuals interested in making a positive impact, we aim to aid a ground-up approach in protecting our environment. Join or host sustainable events for our community or simply enjoy some personal gardening at one of the many community gardens in Singapore.

Singapore's Health Promotion Board recommends gardening as a physical activity that helps to improve flexibility, strength, and mental well-being among Singaporeans.

Over 90% of Singapore's food supply comes from other countries. This reliance on imports contributes to food security challenges.

Sustainability events are part of a broader global movement to address environmental challenges. They align with international goals such as the United Nations sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
